Banana Flapjacks

Description

A fun breakfast recipe that a toddler can help with, it contains no sugar, and she will actually eat it? Sign me up!

For best flavor you want bananas that are really ripe. For bonus points you can thaw bananas from the freezer and then they are super easy for the toddler to mash up!

Ingredients

  • bananas
  • canola oil
  • milk
  • sugar
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. combine wet ingredients, then sift dry ingredients and mix
  2. let rest while the griddle heats up
  3. cook 1/4 cup portions in a little pad of butter
  4. serve and enjoy!
