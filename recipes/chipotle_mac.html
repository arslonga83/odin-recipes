Chipotle Mac

Description

This is one of our favorite weeknight staples. The sauce comes together super quickly in the blender while the pasta is cooking, and it is creamy, spicy, and delicious.

We pretty much always roast brussels to go with it, because that was in the original blog post I found. I guess you could eat this without the brussels, but....why?

Ingredients

  • cashews
  • miso paste
  • garlic
  • nooch
  • veggie broth
  • chipotle peppers
  • pasta

Steps

  1. boil pasta
  2. combine all other ingredients in blender
  3. stir sauce into pasta
  4. eat up!
